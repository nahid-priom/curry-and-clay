About Us

At Curry & Clay Oven, we take pride in offering an authentic Indian dining experience, crafted with passion and tradition. From fragrant biryanis to sizzling tandoori delights, each dish is carefully prepared using the finest ingredients and traditional recipes passed down through generations. Immerse yourself in the warm hospitality of India as you indulge in our diverse menu, featuring an array of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options to tantalize your taste buds. Whether you're craving the fiery spices of North Indian cuisine or the subtle flavors of South Indian delicacies, our menu caters to every palate. Join us for a culinary journey through the heart of India, where every meal is an exploration of taste, culture, and tradition. Experience the essence of India right here at Curry & Clay Oven.
